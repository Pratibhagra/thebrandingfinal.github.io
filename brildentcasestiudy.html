Blackrose Industries Ltd. launched its dental business segment BRILDENT in October 2019. An new importer of a unique dental product SUPER-BOND C&B along with resin cement, composites, bonding agents, primers, and desensitizers from the Japan-based Sun Medical company wanted to establish in the Indian Dental Market.

BRILDENT was a new Brand in India and the challenge was to gain the trust of the dentists and established itself in the dental Industry.

Conclusion

BRILDENT is established in the Indian Market and now a well-known Brand across INDIA Dentists are likely to recommend it in their Daily Dental Practice.
